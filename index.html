How to learn physics ?

2022-12-27 :: Sai Yeswanth
depending on what your source of education is (or who your teacher is ), learning physics can be a rewarding experience as you understand the inner workings of world, or a path full of frustration and demotivating struggle to get the answers right (which most educators would gladly reduce the purpose of physics to). If you find the equations and formulas confusing, and often encounter ambiguity while trying to solve a physics problem, then this post is for you, hang on as we discuss how certain approaches/practices can actually harm the student’s ability to learn and there by significantly reducing their chances at being relevant ( like it is in India ).
